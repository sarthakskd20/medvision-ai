BACKUP TALKING POINTS (For Judge Q&A)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Q: How do you handle liability?
A: "We position as decision support with full transparency. The AI shows its 
   reasoning, cites sources, and the physician makes the final call. Every 
   output is marked as AI-assisted, never AI-diagnosed."

Q: What about data privacy?
A: "HIPAA-ready architecture. Data can stay on-premise. Anonymization before 
   AI processing. We never train on patient data."

Q: Why Gemini 3 specifically?
A: "Context window. Two million tokens means we can hold a decade of patient 
   history in a single inference. GPT-4 maxes at 128K, Claude at 200K. This 
   simply cannot exist with any other model."

Q: How is this different from existing tools?
A: "Existing tools analyze snapshots. We analyze the complete movie. 
   Longitudinal pattern recognition across years of data is our moat."

Q: What's the business model?
A: "SaaS per-physician licensing for hospitals. Freemium patient portal with 
   premium features. Research partnerships for anonymized insights."

Q: Can this work in rural areas?
A: "Cloud-based, works on any browser with internet. Minimal infrastructure. 
   Designed to bring specialist-level insights to underserved communities."
